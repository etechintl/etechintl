Our Story, Mission & Vision

Etech International Pvt. Ltd. was established in August 2015 with a vision to bridge the technology gap in Nepal's rapidly evolving digital landscape. Founded by experienced IT professionals, we have grown from a small startup to one of Nepal's most trusted IT infrastructure providers.

  • Mission: Provide world-class IT infrastructure and security solutions that empower Nepalese organizations to achieve digital transformation with security, reliability, and performance.
  • Vision: Be Nepal's leading IT solutions provider, recognized for innovation, quality, and customer satisfaction.
  • Core Values:
    • Quality First: No compromise on product or service quality.
    • Customer-Centric: Tailored solutions with deep client understanding.
    • Innovation & Excellence: Staying ahead with tech trends and capabilities.
    • Integrity & Trust: Transparency, honesty, and reliability in service.
    • Professional Excellence: Skilled team with passion and dedication.
